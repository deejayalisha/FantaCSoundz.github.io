About Us

FantaCSoundz Entertainment is a group of deejays and remixers from New York in the West Indian community. In this era, most people we know like to hear covers of songs, hear a new beat added into them, or even hear a mashup of their most liked songs. Most of us in the team, remix or do a fullmix of certain songs whether the genre is reggaeton, soca, danehall, indian or hip hop. We also take bookings for gigs such as Weddings, Sweet 16, birthday, etc. We provide a wide range of sound and light equipment for your sepcial occasion!
